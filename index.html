How Does Liquid KI Oral Solution Work?

See How KI Works
The thyroid isn’t able to tell the difference between radioactive and stable iodine—in a nuclear emergency, it will absorb both.
By taking Liquid KI Oral Solution, the thyroid absorbs the stable iodine in the medicine.
The thyroid ‘‘fills up’’ with the stable iodine provided by Liquid KI.
Once the thyroid ‘‘fills up’’ with stable iodine, it cannot absorb anymore iodine—including radioactive iodine—for 24 hours.
